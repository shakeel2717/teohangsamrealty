Menara See Hoy Chan

Menara See Hoy Chan is the ideal building for MNCs and other corporations who wish to be close to the commercial centres and shopping districts of KLCC and Bukit Bintang. The building has over 300,000 sq ft in commercial space, and 3 basement car park levels.

Corporates can choose to rent a whole floor of close to 45,000sq ft or an office unit starting from 1,700 sq ft. There is a 10 storey tower block facing KLCC, and a 6 storey block at rear affording views of lush greenery; units are still available to suit your company's preferred views of the city or surrounds.
